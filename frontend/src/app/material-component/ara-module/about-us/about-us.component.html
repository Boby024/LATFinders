About Ara

ARA (Advanced Review Analyser) is a project from LATFinders group.
A group of students of the course Learning Analytics.
The aim is to make it easier to compare universities and courses with each other and to select the best course easier.
We want to make the decision making for the universities and for the students easier.
We use the data on study check as data source and we use ARIMA Model to predict the trend of ratings.
You can find us under the link of the github project.
